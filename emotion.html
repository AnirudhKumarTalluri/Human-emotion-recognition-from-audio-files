SPEECH EMOTION RECOGNITION

Emotion recognition from speech is a vital area in human-computer interaction. This project focuses on predicting speech emotions using Long Short-Term Memory (LSTM) networks, a type of recurrent neural network (RNN). The study aims to contribute significantly to the field of speech emotion recognition, potentially paving the way for more inclusive and accurate emotion prediction models in real-world applications.
